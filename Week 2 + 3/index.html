Get ready to make a doughnut cos I'm gonna tell you

Follow these steps to the absolute T

  1. connect your phone/pc to good speakers via a wire or bluetooth
  2. open spotify or youtube and play Axel F by Crazy Frog
  3. set it to repeat endlessly
  4. make sure your cooking area is clean
  5. find a good recipe for doughnuts and get out the utensils and ingrediants you need
  6. make the dough
  7. form the balls
  8. deep fry 'em
  9. flip them halways them remove them when done and place on paper towels
  10. fill those bad boys with jam or chocolate
  11. slap some icing on them
  12. make them look pretty and voila
Now remember guys, you don't have to just use jam and chocolote, and there's a whole host of delicous things you can put on top so don't be afrind to venture out of this list that I'm about to show you
  • Jam (Strawberry, Raspberry, Apricot, Blackcurrant etc.)
  • Chocolate (Nutella)
  • Custard (Plain, Vanilla, Banana etc.)
  • Cream
  • Caramel (Salted)
  • Icing (Lemon, Sugar, Raspberry etc.)
  • Sprinkles (Chocolate, Rainbow)
  • Fruit
